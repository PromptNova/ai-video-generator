What is the Custom Script field for?
Switch to "Custom Script" tab and type your own voice-over text. This overrides the auto-generated hook as TTS input, while still generating hooks for A/B testing.
What does the A/B Compare feature do?
Click A/B Compare, then mark two hooks as A and B. They'll be visually highlighted so you can compare them side-by-side before choosing which one to apply.
